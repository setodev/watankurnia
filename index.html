UCaaS

UCaaS stands for "Unified Communications as a Service." It is a cloud-based service model that combines various communication and collaboration tools into a single platform. The goal of UCaaS is to streamline communication and improve collaboration within organizations by integrating different modes of communication, such as voice calls, video conferencing, instant messaging, email, and more.

VoIP

Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) is a technology that enables voice communication and multimedia sessions to be transmitted over the Internet or other IP-based networks. In simpler terms, it allows you to make phone calls using the internet rather than traditional telephone lines.

Structured Cabling

Structured cabling refers to the standardized and organized approach of designing, installing, and managing the network infrastructure within a building or facility. It involves the deployment of a comprehensive system of cabling and associated hardware to support various communication technologies, such as data, voice, video, and other forms of information transmission.
